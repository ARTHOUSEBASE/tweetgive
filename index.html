How It Works

Tweet to Give connects social media generosity with blockchain transparency — no wallet address copying, no complex steps.

The Giving Flow

Step 01

Find a Campaign

Browse verified campaigns on our platform. Each has a .base.eth name and transparent milestones.

Step 02

Compose Your Tweet

Write a personal message to the creator, mention @bankrbot, add #tweetgiveonbase and the campaign ID.

Step 03

@bankrbot Replies

Within seconds, @bankrbot replies to your tweet with a secure payment link for the amount you mentioned.

Step 04

Confirm & Done

Click the link, confirm in your wallet. The donation is recorded on Base — forever visible on-chain.

🔗

On-Chain Transparency

Every donation is a permanent on-chain transaction on Base. No black boxes — donors can always verify where funds went and whether milestones were met.

🤖

@bankrbot Magic

@bankrbot is a social payment bot that listens to tweets with specific hashtags. It automatically generates a payment link and processes the transaction — no app downloads needed.

✅

Milestone Accountability

Campaign creators break their goals into milestones. Funds are only released when progress is proven — photos, receipts, and on-chain verification keep creators accountable.

🌐

Base Ecosystem

Built on Base — Coinbase's L2 for fast, cheap, global transactions. Gas fees are minimal, making micro-donations of even 0.001 ETH practical and meaningful.

🪪

.base.eth Identity

All campaign creators must verify with a .base.eth name — a human-readable on-chain identity. This creates trust and makes donation addresses memorable and verifiable.

💰

Fair Fee Structure

Tweet to Give takes a 3% platform fee to cover operations and @bankrbot integration. 97% of every donation goes directly to the campaign creator's verified wallet.

The Exact Tweet Format

Hey @[creator_handle], [your personal message]. Sending [amount] ETH to help out [emoji] @bankrbot #tweetgiveonbase [campaign-id]

The campaign ID is shown on every campaign page. @bankrbot parses your tweet, identifies the campaign, and generates a secure payment link it replies with. You never need to copy-paste a wallet address.

About Tweet to Give

A new model for social giving on Base

What is Tweet to Give?

Tweet to Give is a decentralized fundraising platform built on Base that makes donating as easy as sending a tweet. We believe giving should be social, transparent, and verifiable — not locked behind complex wallet UX.

By integrating with @bankrbot, we allow anyone with a crypto wallet and an X account to donate to verified campaigns using a simple tweet. No address copying, no confusing interfaces — just a personal message that carries real value.

Why Base?

Base is Coinbase's L2 blockchain — fast, cheap, and built for real-world use. Gas fees are minimal (often under $0.01), making micro-donations practical. All transactions are permanent and publicly verifiable, creating a new standard of accountability for charitable giving.

Our Mission

We're building infrastructure for a world where giving is as frictionless as liking a post, but as verifiable and permanent as a blockchain transaction. Every campaign on Tweet to Give comes with milestone-based accountability — donors always know where their money goes.
