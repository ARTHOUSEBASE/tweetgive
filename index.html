About Tweet to Give

Tweet to Give is a decentralized fundraising platform built on Base that makes donating as easy as tweeting. We leverage @bankrbot to enable social giving with full on-chain transparency.

Every donation is tracked on the blockchain, ensuring 100% transparency in how funds are used.

Key Features:

  • Social media-native donations
  • Verified creator identities via .base.eth
  • Milestone-based fund release
  • Only 3% platform fee
